About the Book

Black History of Maury County celebrates the lives, achievements, and lasting contributions of African Americans throughout Maury County, Tennessee.

Through photographs, historical records, churches, schools, organizations, family histories, and community stories, this publication preserves generations of history that continue to inspire future generations.

This website provides free public access to the digital edition in support of education, historical preservation, and community appreciation.
